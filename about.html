It All Began In A Bar ...

We sat in a bar in Stockholm a cold winter night in 2017, and got enthralled by the beauty of the liquor bottles standing majestically on the shelf. That's when we decided on making a liquor company.
Fast forward half a year, we drove around Funen on Puch Maxi scooters in fitting attire. An idea was born.

What About The Sidecar?

Later that year got to sit in on a motorcyclist, whom of course had a sidecar attached to his motorcycle. And thus, a brand was born. Now we both got our motorcycle license, and if you're lucky, you can see us cruising around the streets of Copenhagen with our delicious Sidevogn Cocktails aboard. *Vrooom!*
